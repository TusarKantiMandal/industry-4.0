About Us

Dana Anand India Pvt. Ltd. is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-performance automotive components. Formed through a strategic joint venture between Dana Incorporated (USA) and the Anand Group (India), the company leverages global innovation and local expertise to deliver reliable driveline systems for a range of vehicles.

We serve leading automotive OEMs in both domestic and global markets, committed to excellence in engineering, quality, and environmental responsibility. Our solutions power mobility in the most demanding conditions, from passenger cars to off-highway applications.

At Dana Anand, we focus on continuous improvement, customer satisfaction, and cutting-edge technologies to remain at the forefront of the automotive supply chain.
